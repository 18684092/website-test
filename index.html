Instructions

Modification: Click the grid squares to turn off or on individual pixels. To select which invader image that you wish to modify click the small image to the top or left of the grid.
Drag n Drop: Click and hold one of the small images of your invader and then drag it over to the main game window. Release to drop.
Controls: Uses the touch screen areas at the bottoms of the screen, or use
keys:
[z] - left
[x] - right
[space] - fire.
